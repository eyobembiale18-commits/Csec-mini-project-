About Me

Hello! I'm Eyob, a digital artist and designer with a passion for creating vibrant, emotionally resonant visuals. I believe color is the most powerful tool in design.

Based in Gondar, I draw inspiration from my cultural heritage, nature, and urban landscapes to create unique digital experiences.

My Skills

I specialize in blending traditional art principles with modern digital techniques to create compelling visual narratives.

Digital Illustration UI/UX Design Brand Identity Web Design 3D Modeling

My work has been featured in design publications and exhibited in galleries across East Africa.

Passions & Interests

When I'm not designing, you can find me exploring local markets for colorful textiles, experimenting with plant-based cooking to find the perfect sunset view.

I'm also passionate about mentoring young designers and regularly volunteer at community art centers.
